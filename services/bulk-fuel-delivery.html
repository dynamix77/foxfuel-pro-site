Bulk Fuel Programs: Market Pricing, Fixed Contracts, and FuelCube Integration

Fox Fuel Pro offers three commercial bulk delivery programs. Choose the pricing structure and service level that aligns with your operational timeline, budget requirements, and fuel sourcing preferences.

Market-Based Delivery

Fuel pricing tied to current market rates with transparent cost-plus or margin structures. Best for customers who want flexibility and are comfortable with market price fluctuations.

Best For:

  • Operations with flexible budgets that can absorb market price changes
  • Customers who prefer spot pricing over long-term commitments
  • Short-term projects (3 to 6 months) where fixed pricing is not critical
  • Businesses that actively manage fuel procurement and market timing

Structure: Per-gallon pricing updated weekly or monthly based on market rates. No long-term contract required. Transparent cost formula disclosed upfront.

Fixed-Price Contracts

Lock in fuel prices for contract terms (3 to 12 months) to provide budget stability and protection against market volatility. Most popular choice for commercial operations.

Best For:

  • Businesses requiring predictable fuel budgets for financial planning
  • Operations with consistent fuel consumption that want price protection
  • Municipal and institutional customers with annual budget cycles
  • Construction projects with fixed-price bids requiring cost certainty

Structure: Fixed per-gallon price for contract duration (typically 3, 6, or 12 months). Scheduled deliveries based on consumption patterns. Volume minimums apply. Early termination fees may apply.

FuelCube Integration Program

Complete fuel management combining FuelCube on-site storage, remote monitoring, and automated bulk delivery scheduling. Eliminate manual fuel monitoring and prevent runouts.

Best For:

  • Fleet operations, construction sites, and facilities with FuelCube on-site tanks
  • Customers who want fully automated fuel delivery without manual reordering
  • Operations requiring real-time tank level visibility and consumption analytics
  • Businesses prioritizing zero-runout reliability and operational continuity

Structure: FuelCube tank (loan, rental, or purchase program) + monitoring service + bulk fuel delivery. Telemetry triggers automated delivery when tank reaches 25 to 35 percent capacity. Pricing includes monitoring fee plus per-gallon fuel cost (market or fixed).

Fuel Types Available for Bulk Delivery

Fox Fuel Pro delivers multiple fuel grades to meet commercial operational requirements. All fuels meet or exceed industry specifications.

Diesel

On-road diesel (ULSD): Ultra-low sulfur diesel meeting ASTM D975 specifications for highway vehicles and light equipment. Suitable for modern diesel engines with emissions controls.

Off-road diesel (dyed): Tax-exempt red-dyed diesel for off-road equipment, construction machinery, generators, and agricultural use. Same ASTM D975 quality standards.

Use cases: Fleet trucks, construction equipment, generators, forklifts, agricultural machinery, heating systems.

Gasoline

Regular unleaded (87 octane): Standard gasoline for light-duty vehicles, generators, and small equipment.

Premium unleaded (91+ octane): Higher octane fuel for performance vehicles and equipment requiring premium grades.

Use cases: Fleet vehicles, utility trucks, portable generators, landscaping equipment, small construction tools.

Heating Oil

Commercial heating oil: ASTM D396 Grade 2 heating oil for commercial and industrial heating applications. Similar to off-road diesel but optimized for heating systems.

Use cases: Industrial facilities, warehouses, manufacturing plants, large commercial buildings with oil-fired heating systems.

Biodiesel & Renewable Diesel

Biodiesel blends (B5, B20): Petroleum diesel blended with biodiesel for operations with sustainability goals or regulatory requirements. ASTM D975 compliant.

Renewable diesel (R99): 100 percent renewable diesel meeting ASTM D975 specifications. Drop-in replacement for petroleum diesel with lower carbon intensity.

Use cases: Government fleets, sustainability-focused operations, California LCFS compliance, corporate ESG initiatives.
