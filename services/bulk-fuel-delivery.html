What Bulk Fuel Delivery Solves

Bulk fuel delivery eliminates the operational drag of retail fuel procurement: no employee time spent making fuel runs, no vehicle downtime waiting at pumps, and no unpredictable retail pricing. Fox Fuel delivers directly to your site or FuelCube storage tanks on scheduled delivery windows.

Fuel Types Available

Fox Fuel delivers multiple commercial fuel grades meeting industry specifications.

  • Diesel (ULSD): On-road and off-road (dyed) for fleets and equipment.
  • Gasoline: Regular (87) and Premium (91+) for light-duty vehicles.
  • Heating Oil: Commercial grade for industrial facility heating.
  • Renewable Diesel: B5-B20 blends and R99 for sustainability goals.
