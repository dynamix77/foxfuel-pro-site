What Jobsite Fueling Solves for Construction Operations

Jobsite fueling eliminates the operational disruption of equipment fuel runs: no deadhead miles to off-site fuel stations, no equipment downtime waiting for fuel deliveries, no manual tank monitoring or delivery coordination. Fox Fuel Pro deploys temporary FuelCube tanks (500, 1000, 2000 gallon) directly at construction and industrial jobsites with automated monitoring and bulk delivery—keeping equipment fueled on-site 24/7 without procurement delays or operator downtime.

Operational Problems Jobsite Fueling Eliminates

  • Equipment deadhead miles: Operators leaving jobsite to fuel equipment at retail stations instead of working, adding non-billable hours and route delays
  • Jobsite downtime: Equipment sitting idle waiting for fuel deliveries or operators returning from off-site fuel runs
  • Manual fuel coordination: Project managers coordinating fuel deliveries, checking tank levels, and scheduling refills manually
  • Fuel runout risk: Running out of on-site fuel during critical construction phases causing project delays and missed deadlines
  • Project relocation complexity: Moving fuel infrastructure between jobsite phases or locations without flexible tank solutions
