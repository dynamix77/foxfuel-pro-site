What Fleet Fueling Solves for Fleet Operations

Fleet fueling eliminates the operational drain of driver fuel runs: no vehicle downtime waiting at retail stations, no lost productivity from fuel procurement trips, no unpredictable retail pricing affecting fleet budgets. Fox Fuel Pro delivers diesel and gasoline in bulk to your fleet yard storage tanks or FuelCube installations with automated monitoring and volume pricing—keeping your tanks full so drivers fuel on-site 24/7 without route delays.

Operational Problems Fleet Fueling Eliminates

  • Driver downtime: Hours per week lost sending drivers to retail fuel stations instead of running routes or making deliveries
  • Route inefficiency: Detours to fuel stations adding unnecessary miles, time delays, and reducing on-time performance
  • Retail fuel costs: Paying retail prices instead of commercial bulk pricing with volume discounts
  • Fuel runout risk: Running out of on-site fuel during peak operations causing vehicle delays and dispatch disruptions
  • Manual tank monitoring: Operations staff spending time checking fuel levels and coordinating deliveries manually

What Fox Fuel Pro Fleet Fueling Provides

  • Bulk delivery to on-site tanks: Scheduled fuel deliveries directly to your fleet yard storage tanks or FuelCube installations
  • 24/7 on-site fuel access: Drivers fuel at your yard any time without retail station dependence or time constraints
  • Automated monitoring & alerts: Real-time telemetry triggers delivery when tanks reach threshold (typically 25 to 35 percent remaining)
  • Volume pricing: Bulk fuel purchasing reduces per-gallon costs compared to retail with contract pricing options
  • FuelCube integration: Portable tank installations (500, 1000, 2000 gallon) with monitoring for yards without existing storage
  • Multi-site coordination: Manage fuel across multiple fleet locations from centralized monitoring dashboard
