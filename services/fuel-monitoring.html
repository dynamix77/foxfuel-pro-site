From Reactive to Proactive

Fuel monitoring transforms reactive fuel management into proactive operational control. Fox Fuel's telemetry systems provide real-time tank level data, consumption trending, and automated delivery scheduling—preventing costly runouts and enabling data-driven decisions.

System Capabilities

Real-Time Visibility

View current fuel levels for all monitored tanks from a single dashboard available 24/7.

Automated Alerts

Receive SMS or email notifications when tanks reach reorder thresholds.

Auto-Replenishment

Dispatch receives alerts automatically to schedule proactive refills.

Usage Analytics

Track consumption patterns to identify usage spikes or potential theft.

Multi-Site View

Monitor unlimited tanks across multiple different locations in one view.

Mobile Access

Access dashboard and alerts from any mobile device, anywhere.

On-Site Tanks + Monitoring

While monitoring works on any tank, it is most powerful when paired with our on-site tank programs. Every tank can be equipped with integrated telemetry.
