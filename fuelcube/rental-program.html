FuelCube Rental Program

Month-to-month flexibility with no long-term commitment. Source your fuel from anywhere while securing compliant, double-wall storage on your site.

Get Rental Pricing View Models

Total Flexibility

Our Rental Program is designed for projects with defined timelines (3-12 months) or uncertain durations. You pay a simple monthly equipment fee and retain the freedom to purchase fuel from any supplier—Fox Fuel, competitors, or retail.

How the Rental Program Works

01

Select & Quote

Choose your tank size (500, 1000, 2000). We provide a monthly rental rate including maintenance. Optional monitoring available.

02

Deploy

We deliver and set up the compliant tank on your site in 3-7 days. You are ready to fuel immediately upon arrival.

03

Fuel Your Way

Order fuel from Fox Fuel Pro or any other supplier. You have total control over your fuel sourcing strategy.

04

Month-to-Month

Keep the tank as long as you need. No long-term lock-in. Ideal for seasonal or project-based work.

05

Easy Return

When the job is done, just give us 30 days notice. We pick up the tank and refund your deposit.

Why Rent?

  • ✓ No Capex: Avoid capital expenditure and keep cash flow free for operations.
  • ✓ Zero Fuel Lock-in: You aren't tied to a specific fuel vendor contract.
  • ✓ Maintenance Included: We handle pump testing and repairs.
  • ✓ Compliance Solved: We provide the UL142 compliant tank and documentation.
