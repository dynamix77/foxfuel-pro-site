Rental Program Process

1

Site Assessment and Rental Quote

Fox Fuel evaluates site for FuelCube placement. We provide monthly rental quote based on tank size and optional services (monitoring, delivery).

2

Rental Agreement and Deposit

Month-to-month rental agreement specifying equipment, monthly fee, and optional services. Refundable deposit typically required (equipment value protection).

3

FuelCube Delivery and Setup

Fox Fuel delivers and positions FuelCube. Pump cabinet installed, safety equipment configured. Customer responsible for initial fuel fill from chosen supplier.

4

Operation and Fuel Management

Customer manages fuel procurement from any supplier. Fox Fuel provides equipment maintenance and compliance support per rental agreement.

5

Rental Conclusion and Removal

At project end, Fox Fuel removes equipment. Deposit refunded after equipment inspection (deductions for damage beyond normal wear). No long-term obligation.

Customer Responsibilities

  • Pay monthly rental fee (typically due at month start)
  • Provide site access and suitable placement location
  • Source and manage fuel procurement (any supplier)
  • Secure pump cabinet and prevent unauthorized access
  • Report equipment issues or damage promptly
