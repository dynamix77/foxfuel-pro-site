Built for Strict Regulations

Fox Fuel Pro ensures your on-site storage meets all North American fuel storage regulations. From double-wall construction to interstitial leak detection, the FuelCube is engineered to satisfy fire marshals and environmental inspectors.

Core Compliance Standards

UL142 Listed

Standard for Steel Aboveground Tanks

Certified double-wall construction. The inner tank holds the fuel, while the outer tank provides 110% containment capacity, ensuring zero ground contamination risks.

NFPA 30 Compliant

Flammable and Combustible Liquids Code

Meets strict requirements for venting, pressure relief, and emergency shutoff valves. Designed to prevent pressure buildup and manage fire risks safely.

EPA SPCC Ready

Spill Prevention, Control, and Countermeasure

The integral secondary containment simplifies your SPCC plan. No external berms or dikes required—containment is built directly into the unit.

UN / DOT Approved

Transport of Dangerous Goods

Select FuelCube models are UN approved for transport while full, allowing you to move fuel across public roads to follow your fleet or project.

Environmental Protection

Protecting your site from spills is our priority.

  • 110% Containment: Captures any leak from the primary tank plus rain ingress.
  • Secure Cabinet: Locks pumps and nozzles behind steel doors to prevent authorized use or vandalism.
  • Drip Catchment: Integrated drip pans catch minor drips during connection/disconnection.
  • Monitoring: Optional telemetry detects sudden level drops indicative of leaks.
