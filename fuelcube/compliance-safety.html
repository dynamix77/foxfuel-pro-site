FuelCube Compliance Standards

All Western Global FuelCube tanks meet or exceed North American fuel storage regulations: UL142 double-wall construction, NFPA 30 flammable liquids code, EPA environmental protection requirements, and state/local fire marshal regulations.

Compliance Requirements Met by FuelCube

UL142 Double-Wall Construction

Standard: Underwriters Laboratories Standard for Steel Aboveground Tanks

  • Primary steel tank (inner wall) holds fuel
  • Secondary steel tank (outer wall) provides 110% containment capacity
  • Interstitial space with leak detection capability
  • Third-party UL142 certification and labeling

NFPA 30 Flammable Liquids Code

Standard: National Fire Protection Association code for flammable liquids storage

  • Meets portable tank classification for diesel fuel storage
  • Integrated pump cabinet with emergency shutoff
  • Proper venting and pressure relief systems
  • Fire-resistant construction and materials

State and Local Fire Marshal Requirements

  • Local permit application guidance and documentation
  • Setback distance recommendations based on local codes
  • Inspection scheduling and compliance verification
  • Documentation for fire marshal review and approval
