Purchase Program Overview

Buy Western Global FuelCube tank and equipment for one-time capital investment. You own the asset, depreciate it on your balance sheet, and control all fuel sourcing decisions. Fox Fuel provides optional delivery, monitoring, and maintenance services with no long-term commitments.

Who Benefits from Purchase

  • Permanent facilities (manufacturing plants, distribution centers)
  • Long-term operations (10+ year timeline)
  • Businesses seeking capital asset ownership
  • Companies with tax depreciation strategies
  • Operations with existing fuel supplier relationships

Why Purchase vs. Loan or Rental

  • vs. Loan: No fuel commitment, own equipment outright, tax depreciation benefits
  • vs. Rental: Lower total cost over 3+ years, no ongoing monthly fees
  • Best for: Permanent installations with 10+ year operational horizon
  • Cost structure: One-time purchase price plus optional service fees

Purchase Process

1

Equipment Selection and Quote

Fox Fuel assesses your needs and recommends FuelCube model. Provides purchase quote including equipment, delivery, and optional services.

2

Purchase Order and Payment

Customer issues purchase order. Payment terms negotiable (typically 50% deposit, 50% on delivery or net 30 for established accounts).

3

Delivery and Placement

FuelCube delivered to site. Fox Fuel positions tank and performs basic setup. Professional installation available (recommended for compliance).

4

Optional Service Setup

If monitoring or delivery services selected, Fox Fuel installs telemetry, configures system, and establishes fuel supply agreement.

5

Ongoing Operation

Customer owns and operates equipment. Fox Fuel provides fuel delivery, monitoring, or maintenance services per service agreements (optional, no long-term commitment).
