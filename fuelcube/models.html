How to Choose the Right FuelCube Model

FuelCube model selection determines your operational rhythm: how many days between refills, how often you are coordinating deliveries, and whether you have fuel buffer for unexpected consumption spikes. Fox Fuel Pro helps you choose the model that balances capacity, site footprint, and refill frequency for your specific operation.

Key Decision Inputs

  • Daily fuel consumption: Total gallons consumed across all equipment and vehicles per day (peak, not average)
  • Desired refill frequency: Preferred days between deliveries (7 to 14 day target for most operations)
  • Equipment count and types: Number of vehicles, heavy equipment units, generators, forklifts fueled from tank
  • Site constraints: Ground conditions, delivery truck access, footprint available, local setback requirements
  • Project or operational timeline: Short term projects versus permanent installations versus seasonal operations

Why Model Sizing Matters

Undersized tanks: Require frequent deliveries (increased delivery costs), higher runout risk, operational disruption from constant coordination.

Oversized tanks: Unnecessary capital or rental costs, excess site footprint, fuel aging concerns for low consumption operations.

Right sized tanks: Optimal 10 to 14 day refill cycle, cost efficiency, operational continuity, buffer for consumption spikes.

FuelCube Acquisition Programs: Loan, Rental, Purchase

Every FuelCube model (500, 1000, 2000) is available through three commercial programs. Choose the model that fits your operations, then choose the program structure that fits your financial and timeline requirements.

Loan Program

Fox Fuel Pro provides FuelCube at no upfront cost in exchange for exclusive fuel supply agreement. Equipment loan paired with ongoing fuel delivery service.

Best For:

  • Long term operations (12 or more months) with consistent fuel consumption
  • Customers prioritizing zero capital outlay and operational fuel expense
  • Operations valuing integrated fuel supply and equipment in a single program

Structure: No equipment purchase or rental fee. Fuel supplied by Fox Fuel Pro at competitive per gallon pricing. Equipment returned at program conclusion.

Learn More: Loan Program Details

Rental Program

Month to month FuelCube rental with no fuel commitment. Customer sources fuel from any supplier, including Fox Fuel Pro, competitors, or retail.

Best For:

  • Short term projects with defined timelines (3 to 12 months)
  • Customers with existing fuel supplier contracts or relationships
  • Seasonal operations requiring flexibility to scale up or down
  • Operations with uncertain fuel volume or project duration

Structure: Monthly rental fee based on model size (300 to 1,000 dollars per month typical range). Customer manages fuel procurement from any source. Cancel with 30 day notice.

Learn More: Rental Program Details

Purchase Program

Buy FuelCube outright as capital asset. Own equipment, depreciate on balance sheet, source fuel from any supplier with zero ongoing obligations.

Best For:

  • Permanent facilities and long term operations (10 or more years)
  • Companies seeking equipment ownership and tax depreciation benefits
  • Operations with existing fuel supplier relationships and no interest in bundled programs
  • Businesses prioritizing asset control and lowest total cost over time

Structure: One time purchase (about 12,000 to 35,000 dollars depending on model). Optional fuel delivery and monitoring services available with no long term commitment. Equipment resale value retained.
