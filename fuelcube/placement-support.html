We Handle the Logistics

From site discovery to compliant deployment, we transform fuel storage from a complex problem into a guided process. We ensure your FuelCube is positioned correctly, compliant with local codes, and ready for operation from day one.

Deployment Process

01

Site Assessment

We evaluate your site for safe access, ground stability, and regulatory setbacks (distance from buildings/property lines).

02

Placement Plan

We provide a clear diagram of where the tank will go, ensuring delivery trucks can access it easily.

03

Permitting Guidance

We don't just drop the tank; we provide the UL142 docs and specs you need for your local fire marshal application.

04

Installation

Our team coordinates the delivery, offloading (crane or forklift), and positioning of the tank.

05

Commissioning

We install pumps, test the system, and train your staff on safe fueling procedures.

06

Ongoing Support

Maintenance, pump repairs, and optional fuel delivery and monitoring services.

Clear Division of Duties

Fox Fuel Pro Handles:

  • Site assessment & recommendations
  • Delivery & positioning
  • Pump installation & testing
  • Initial operator training
  • Monitoring installation (if selected)

You Handle:

  • Site prep (level ground/pad)
  • Permit applications (with our docs)
  • Site security (fencing if needed)
  • Electrical power (for AC pumps)
