Fueling Challenges in Scrap Yards

  • Site Hazards: Debris and sharp metal make mobile fueling risky for standard trucks (tire damage). Safe fueling zones are critical.
  • Heavy Consumption: Material handlers and shears burn fuel constantly. Running dry stops the entire processing line.
  • Equipment Durability: Dust, grime, and vibration destroy cheap pumps. You need industrial-grade fueling equipment.

How Fox Fuel Solves This

Safe-Zone Delivery

We deliver to a designated clean zone tank, keeping our trucks away from the "hot zone" of the scrap pile.

Wet Hosing

For accessible equipment, we can refuel directly during lunch breaks or shift changes to maximize uptime.

Off-Road Diesel

Save money with tax-exempt red-dyed diesel for your non-road yard equipment and machinery.

Tough Tanks for Tough Sites

Get a double-walled fuel tank that can handle the grit of a scrap yard environment.

  • Double-wall construction for environmental safety
  • Forklift pockets for easy relocation in the yard
  • Secure locking mechanism prevents fuel theft
