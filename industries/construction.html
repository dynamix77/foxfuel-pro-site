Fueling Challenges in Construction

  • Equipment Downtime: Every hour a machine sits idle waiting for fuel costs you productivity and profit.
  • Off-Site Fuel Runs: Paying operators to drive machines or trucks to a gas station burns billable hours.
  • Inventory Shrinkage: Without proper monitoring, fuel theft and unaccounted usage eat into project margins.
  • Remote Access: Getting fuel trucks to difficult terrain or early-stage excavation sites can be unreliable.

How Fox Fuel Solves This

Jobsite Fueling

We deliver diesel directly to your site, refilling on-site tanks so you start every shift full.

On-Site Storage

We deploy double-wall FuelCube tanks (500-2,000 gal) to your site, giving you a secure, private fuel station.

Monitoring & Data

Remote tank monitoring allows us to refill automatically, while our reporting helps you track fuel costs per project.
