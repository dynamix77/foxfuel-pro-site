The Reality

Fleet Fuel Challenges We Solve

Drivers at Gas Stations

Every hour your driver spends at a fuel pump is an hour off the road. Multiply that across your fleet, across shifts, across the year. The productivity loss adds up fast. Gas station detours are not free.

Impact: Lost revenue miles. Driver time wasted.

Unpredictable Fuel Costs

Drivers fueling at retail stations pay whatever price is posted. Different stations, different prices, different receipts to reconcile. No visibility into actual per-vehicle consumption without manual tracking.

Impact: Budget variance. Tracking burden.

Morning Dispatch Delays

Trucks that left yesterday with half tanks need fuel before today's routes. Drivers waiting for fuel delays the whole dispatch. The schedule backs up before the first stop.

Impact: Late departures. Compressed routes.

DEF Management

Modern diesel trucks require DEF. Managing DEF alongside diesel adds complexity. Running low on DEF triggers engine derate. Another consumable to track, another stop to make.

Impact: Derate risk. Additional logistics.
