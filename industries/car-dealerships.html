Fueling Challenges for Dealerships

  • Wasted Labor: Every trip a porter or salesperson takes to a retail gas station is time they aren't helping customers.
  • Lot Congestion: Moving cars in and out just for fuel creates unnecessary traffic and potential for lot damage.
  • Receipt Nightmares: Tracking dozens of petty cash receipts or credit card swipes is an accounting headache.

How Fox Fuel Solves This

On-Site Fueling

Install a FuelCube on your lot so vehicles can be fueled immediately after PDI or before a test drive.

Service Fleet Fueling

We keep your parts trucks, shuttles, and loaner cars full so they are always ready for service.

Simplified Billing

One monthly invoice for all your fuel, broken down by department or vehicle if integrated with our monitoring.

Compact Storage for Tight Lots

You don't need a massive area to have your own fuel station. The FuelCube is designed for space efficiency.

  • Small footprint (fits in a standard parking space)
  • Aesthetically clean design (doesn't look like an industrial tank)
  • Protects inventory from retail pump damage risks
  • Lockable to prevent unauthorized use after hours
