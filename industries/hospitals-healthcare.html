Fueling Challenges for Healthcare

  • Regulatory Compliance: Meeting JCAHO and NFPA standards for fuel quality and generator testing is mandatory, not optional.
  • Emergency Readiness: In a natural disaster, hospitals are the first line of defense. You cannot afford to wait in line for fuel.
