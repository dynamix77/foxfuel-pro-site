Fueling Challenges in Mining

  • Rough Access: Getting fuel trucks down into the pit or up to remote crushers requires specialized drivers and vehicles.
  • High Consumption: Large haul trucks burn massive amounts of fuel. Running out means halting production for the entire site.
  • Harsh Environments: Dust, vibration, and weather take a toll on standard fuel pumps and tanks.

How Fox Fuel Solves This

Direct-to-Pit Delivery

Our off-road capable trucks deliver directly to equipment in the pit, saving you time hauling fuel from the top.

Wet Hosing

We refill excavators, loaders, and stationary crushers during shift changes or downtime to maximize operating hours.

Rugged Storage

We deploy heavy-duty FuelCube tanks designed to withstand the vibration and rough conditions of active quarries.
