Fueling Challenges in Transit

  • Driver Downtime: Paying drivers to wait in line at retail pumps eats into your route margins and service hours.
  • Route Scheduling: Unexpected fuel stops cause delays that ripple through your entire daily schedule.
  • Theft & Tracking: Retail card misuse and untracked gallons make it hard to control operating costs.

How Fox Fuel Solves This

Yard Fueling

We deliver in bulk to your yard tanks, allowing drivers to fuel up immediately before or after their shifts.

Fleet Fueling

For eligible fleets, we can provide direct-to-vehicle "wet hosing" overnight, so every bus starts the morning full.

FuelCube Solutions

Install a smart, tracked fuel station right in your depot to monitor usage by vehicle and driver automatically.

Centralized Yard Fueling

Turn your parking lot into a private fuel station. A FuelCube gives you control over every gallon.

  • High-flow pumps for faster fill times
  • Card-lock or code access for driver accountability
  • Compact footprint fits in crowded yards
  • Eliminates off-route miles to gas stations
