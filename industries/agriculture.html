Fueling Challenges in Agriculture

  • Critical Windows: When weather permits, you need to run 24/7. Empty tanks during harvest can cost thousands per hour.
  • Remote Fields: Moving slow equipment back to the barn for fuel wastes valuable daylight and labor.
  • Price Volatility: Buying fuel at the peak of the season without a strategy hurts margins.

How Fox Fuel Solves This

Direct-to-Field Delivery

We deliver bulk diesel and gas directly to your farm tanks or field-side equipment, even in remote locations.

Seasonal Support

We ramp up deliveries during planting and harvest seasons to ensure you never run dry when it matters most.

Storage Solutions

Deployment of additional temporary tanks during peak seasons to increase your on-farm buffer capacity.

Fuel Right in the Field

Don't waste time driving equipment back to the main tanks. Place a FuelCube directly at the field edge.

  • Rugged double-wall design withstands field conditions
  • Solar/battery pump options for off-grid power
  • Lockable cabinet prevents fuel theft
