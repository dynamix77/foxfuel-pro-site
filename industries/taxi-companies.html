Fueling Challenges for Taxi Fleets

  • Driver Downtime: Every minute a driver spends finding a gas station or waiting in line is a lost fare.
  • Fraud & Theft: Retail fuel cards are prone to skimming and unauthorized purchases (snacks, cigarettes, etc.).
  • Usage Tracking: Manually reconciling hundreds of paper receipts from different stations is a nightmare for accounting.

How Fox Fuel Solves This

Private Fuel Depot
