Fueling Challenges for Power Plants

  • Zero Fail-Rate: When the grid demands power, your turbines must spin. Fuel supply interruptions are simply not an option.
  • Massive Consumption: During extended outages, plants can burn through fuel reserves in hours, requiring constant tanker rotation.
  • Fuel Quality: Stored diesel for backup systems must be tested and polished regularly to prevent injector failure during startup.
